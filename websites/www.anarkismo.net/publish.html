Please use this form to contribute new articles and ideas. The Editorial Group will review your proposal in order to decide if it is in agreement with our editorial policy.

Comments are part of the discussion on articles - if you wish to comment on any article on this site, please use the "Add Comment" link at the end of the article in question. Comments appear on the site before they are reviewed by the Editorial Group.

Please note:
If you have a question or request for our Editorial Group, please use the Contact Form instead of submitting an article with this form.

Editorial Policy
Anarkismo.net is an international anarchist-comunista news service. We are interested in real stories, news and opinions from the anarchist-communist movement around the world. Other texts may not be published, depending on the volume of articles on the site and our Editorial Policy. For further information, see our Editorial Statement.
